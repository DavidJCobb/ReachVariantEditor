Map and Game Settings

The map and game settings page allows you to edit settings related to what objects can spawn on the map, and how powerups work.

The fields available to edit are as follows:

Grenades on Map
Controls whether grenades can spawn on the map.
Shortcuts on Map
Unknown.
Armor Abilities on Map
Controls whether Armor Ability pickups can spawn on the map.
Powerups on Map
Controls whether powerups can spawn on the map.
Turrets on Map
Controls whether turrets can spawn on the map.
Indestructible Vehicles
Controls whether vehicles can be destroyed.
Weapon Set
Controls which types of weapons can spawn on the map. Spawn points for ineligible weapons may be altered to produce an eligible weapon, or may produce nothing, depending on the chosen weapon set.
Vehicle Set
Controls which types of vehicles can spawn on the map. Spawn points for ineligible vehicles may be altered to produce an eligible vehicle, or may produce nothing, depending on the chosen vehicle set.
Red Powerup Duration
Controls how long the player has Red Powerup Traits after picking up an Overshield powerup.
Blue Powerup Duration
Controls how long the player has Blue Powerup Traits after picking up an Active Camo powerup.
Custom Powerup Duration
Controls how long the player has Yellow Powerup Traits after picking up a Custom Powerup.
